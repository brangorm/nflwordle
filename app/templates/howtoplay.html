How to play NFL Wordle

General Rules

This is NFL Wordle, a spinoff of a popular word-guessing game. Instead of guessing words, you will guess names of NFL players.
For each game, a secret NFL player is generated and it is your job to guess the name of the player within 6 tries.
You can figure out who the player is based on comparisons between your guesses and the player, which the game gives to you.

Rules for guessing

Your guess may be any player in the pool of possible secret players. Here are the guidelines:
1. Possible positions: QB, WR, RB, TE, K
2. Scored at least 5 fantasy points in 2021
3. Capitalization/punctuation doesn't matter, just spell the names right.

The Stat column

The stat column means something different depending on the secret player's position on the field.
 If the secret player is a QB, this column will represent the passing yards of each QB you guess.
 If the secret player is a RB, this column will represent the rushing yards of each RB you guess.
 If the secret player is a WR, this column will represent the receiving yards of each WR you guess.
 If the secret player is a TE, this column will represent the receiving yards of each TE you guess.
 If the secret player is a kicker, this column will represent the total fantasy points of each kicker you guess.
The stat column will only be revealed once you guess a player with the correct position.
See below for what the color of this column means.

Color Codes

1. Red: This piece of information is not correct.
2. Green: This piece of information is exactly correct.
3. Yellow: This piece of information is almost correct. Could be either of the following:
 a. The team is in the correct division, but is not the correct team.
 b. The secret player used to play for this team but no longer plays there.
4. Orange: This piece of information is too high. Could be either of the following:
 a. Your player's Stat column is higher than that of the secret player.
 b. Your guess is older than the secret player (if in the Age column).
 c. Your guess is taller than the secret player (if in the Height column).
5. Blue: This piece of information is too low. Could be either of the following:
 a. Your player's Stat column is lower than that of the secret player.
 b. Your guess is younger than the secret player (if in the Age column).
 c. Your guess is shorter than the secret player (if in the Height column).

FAQ

1. I entered Michael Thomas and it said he's not a valid player!
 The pool of available players includes the 508 NFL players who scored 5 or more fantasy points in 2021.
 Since Michael Thomas (and many other talented players) did not play in 2021, they are not in the pool of answers
 and will be treated as invalid guesses. Ironically, Mike Thomas (the Bengals WR) is a valid player. Maybe try him.
2. Why did the first row of my table suddenly turn red?
 This means that the answer is in the bottom 20% of the pool of players. It is a warning that this will likely
 be a tough player to guess.
